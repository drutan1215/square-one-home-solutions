What We Specialize In

We focus on the residential renovation work that matters most to homeowners in Northeast Ohio.

🛁

Bathroom Remodeling

Full bathroom transformations — tile, fixtures, showers, vanities, and everything in between. We handle gut renovations and cosmetic upgrades alike.

🏗️

Basement Build-Outs

Turn your unfinished basement into usable, beautiful living space. Framing, drywall, flooring, electrical coordination, and trim — all managed by Square One.

🎨

Interior Painting

From single rooms to whole-home refreshes, we prep and paint to a professional standard that lasts. Straight lines, no drips, no mess left behind.

Drywall Repair & Installation

Invisible patches, new wall installation, ceiling repairs. We match existing texture perfectly so repairs are undetectable.
